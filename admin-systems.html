Important

Managed does not mean uncontrolled automation.

  • Customer and supplier communications remain human-reviewed until explicitly approved.
  • Payments and financial approvals stay with authorised people.
  • Protected records stay inside approved private workspaces.
  • Automation is added only after the manual workflow produces reliable outcomes.
  • TAD reports what happened; it does not invent savings or financial results.
