# No-Sell Until Validated

The Admin Department must not sell the admin systems as working systems until validation gates are passed.

## Allowed now

We can say:

- We are building admin systems.
- We have demo workflows.
- We can run an internal audit.
- We can test one workflow with synthetic or anonymised data.

## Not allowed yet

Do not say:

- The systems are fully working.
- The systems are proven.
- The systems automate your admin.
- The systems are ready for client data.
- The systems are ready for paid pilot.

## Current product status

- Invoice Admin: logic-testable, not live-sheet tested.
- Sales Admin: prototype only.
- Client Admin: prototype only.

## First sellable milestone

Invoice Admin becomes sellable as a controlled paid pilot only after:

- live Google Sheet formulas work
- 10 test records pass/fail correctly
- weekly report calculates from tracker data
- privacy/access rules are in place
- handover test passes

## Reason

Trust matters more than speed.

Selling before validation would create client delivery risk and damage the brand.
