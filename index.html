How the service works

We do not hand you an empty app and disappear.

  1. 01 · Diagnose. The Admin Audit identifies the first workflow, top control leaks and baseline.
  2. 02 · Install. We configure the controlled list, statuses, owners, dates, approval boundary and private TAD Client Portal workspace.
  3. 03 · Organise. We clean and load the agreed starting records within the published active-record boundary.
  4. 04 · Stabilise. TAD runs the real queue for 14 days, corrects data quality and escalates human decisions.
  5. 05 · Prove and decide. Reports show received, moved, completed, overdue, blocked and scope pressure so the client can Continue, Change or Stop.

What happens after you apply

Application is not instant activation.

  1. 01 · Private review. Your readiness application enters TAD Admin HQ for human review.
  2. 02 · Scope and payment. We confirm the workflow boundary, active-record limit, authority, price and start date. Payment is handled manually before implementation.
  3. 03 · Workspace creation. A qualified application is converted into the correct private managed workspace.
  4. 04 · Portal activation. TAD sends the confirmed primary contact a Client Portal activation link. The client must use the same verified email supplied in the application.
  5. 05 · Controlled setup. We clean and load the agreed backlog, confirm owners, next actions, dates, approval rules and the first report date.
  6. 06 · 14-day stabilisation. Day 3 checks data quality, Day 7 provides the first report and Day 14 produces a Continue, Change or Stop recommendation.
