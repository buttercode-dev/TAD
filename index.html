How the service works

We do not hand you an empty app and disappear.

  1. 01 · Diagnose. The Admin Audit identifies the first workflow, missing controls and baseline measures.
  2. 02 · Install. We configure the tracker, statuses, owners, dates, templates, approval rules and private TAD Client Portal workspace.
  3. 03 · Organise. We clean and import the starting records so the system begins with the real backlog.
  4. 04 · Operate. TAD checks the queue, records outcomes and escalates human decisions.
  5. 05 · Prove and improve. Weekly reports show the work moved, rolled over and blocked.
